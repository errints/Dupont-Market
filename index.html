Menu and Grocery







Our Story



We opened back in the 1990s and are one of the few longstanding, established neighborhood haunts of Dupont.

We are a grocery store, deli, and Wine, Beer & Spirits retailer.

Some say we are one of Dupont Circle's best kept secrets. We can't confirm or deny,
but we can say that we are one of the few places where you can find a very elusive D.C. menu item:
the breakfast burrito.

Speaking of, did we mention that we have brunch all day?!

Come see for yourself - we can't wait to meet you!
