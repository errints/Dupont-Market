Bagel Sandwiches
Pick your spreads and toppings served on toasted Whatsabagels

Egg Sandwiches
Endless options of breads and bagels; eggs fried, scrambled or just whites; assorted cheeses and fresh toppings will have you feeling like you made your customized bagel yourself without getting out of bed

Cheesesteaks
Several Styles of Cheesesteaks for you to choose from.

finally choose to top it with one of a variety of cheeses
Options include: Buffalo Chicken Cheesesteak, Cheesesteak Italiano, Korean Bulgogi Hoagie, Traditional Philly Cheesesteak(kind of)


Burritos & Wraps
Choice of White, Wheat or Spinach Tortilla.
Options include: Beef burrito, Kevin's special (turkey), Veggie Burrito, Chicken Burrito, Parmesan Ranch Chicken Wrap, and more


Salads
Our Salads are huge and would be considered an entire meal for 1 or can easily shared between two or even three people as a side. The best salads are evenly coated with dressing so we have made our salads easy to mix in our large containers. Just add dressing, carefully hold container closed and shake to your hearts content **with sealed side of container facing you just in case, then eat from container or pour in a bowl or on a plate.
Options include: Greek Salad (traditional or add Chicken), House Garden Salad


Chips, Dips & Snacks
Homemade Salsa, Hummus, Jalapeno Feta Spread, Dolmas, Desert Pepper Salsas and a huge variety of Potato chips and tortilla chips..
